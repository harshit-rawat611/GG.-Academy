CBSE Class 12th Syllabus and Pattern 2020-21.

18 Dec, 2020

Latest CBSE Class 12th Syllabus 2020-21 Hindi,English, Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Political Science, Geography, Accountancy, Business Studies, Psychology and other subjects.

CBSE has released the class 12th syllabus for session 2020-21. There is no change in the pattern of the exam, it is same as the previous year. The Board examination will be conducted by CBSE at the end of the year.

  • The pattern of Clas Xl for the Periodic Test and Half Yearly will be followed in a Class Xll also.
  • There will be no regional level and school level Pre Board examination for practise of students as usual.
  • In Class Xll CBSE guidelines will be followed and there will be CBSE examination in Feb/March.
